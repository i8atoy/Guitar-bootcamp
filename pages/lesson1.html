Module Overview

Deep within the heart of an ancient, mist-shrouded forest, the gnarled roots of towering oaks weave a complex tapestry across the mossy earth. Sunlight filters through the dense canopy in shimmering shafts of gold, illuminating tiny particles of dust dancing in the cool, damp air. A hidden stream trickles nearby, its gentle bubbling providing a rhythmic soundtrack to the rustle of emerald leaves. Here, time seems to slow down, caught in the quiet stillness of nature’s cathedral. It is a place where legends feel tangible and the frantic pace of the modern world fades into a distant, forgotten memory of noise.
